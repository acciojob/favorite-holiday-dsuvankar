Kolkata


Kolkata, the capital of the Indian state of West Bengal, is known for its grand colonial architecture, art galleries, and cultural festivals. It is a vibrant city that showcases a blend of traditional and modern culture.

  1. The Victoria Memorial - A magnificent white marble museum dedicated to Queen Victoria.
  2. Howrah Bridge - An iconic bridge that spans the Hooghly River, connecting Kolkata with Howrah.
  3. Indian Museum - The largest and oldest museum in India, featuring a vast collection of artifacts.
  4. Kalighat Temple - A famous Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Kali.
  5. Park Street - A bustling street known for its restaurants, bars, and nightlife.
  • Rasgulla - A traditional Bengali dessert made from chhena and semolina dough, cooked in sugar syrup.
  • Kathi Roll - A popular street food consisting of kebabs wrapped in paratha bread.
  • Fish Curry - A staple dish in Bengali cuisine, often made with hilsa fish.
  • Sandesh - A classic Bengali sweet made from fresh paneer and sugar.
  • Macher Jhol - A spicy fish stew, typically served with rice.
